So who are we?
Happening. is an event organisation created by and for the lovers of electronic music and exceptional parties. Happening. was founded by international students residing in the city of The Hague in March 2016 Happening. is a new, diverse concept aiming to bring only the best of electronic music to the masses, through unique events.
